Let us Learn, Share and Envision
A Vision for Science Teaching and Learning Built on Research




President of SYSM Professor Nakeebullah is giving interview

Society of Young Mathematical Scientists (SYMS) is mainly established for the students of Pakistan and especially Balochistan. The reason for society title is: SYMS team believes that a person can be a scientist if he has good knownledge of science subjects(physics,mathematics,chemistry or biology).

Objectives

Awareness Programs

We will provide a platform for latest information regarding fields of study, Scholarship programs and internships through workshops, seminars and online mentors on the website.

True Sprit of Education + Confidence

We focus on providing different platforms such as presentations, Quiz competitions, speeches and fruitful discussions for the students to boost their confidence and change their attitude accordingly.

Making young Innovative Scientists

We aim to give true vision of science and mathematics to the students. We also aim to produce young innovative scholars by setting up important events such as science exhibitions, programs on real life applications of science and math.

Helping Underprivileged Students

We will raise funds for deserving students by conducting programs, involving dignitaries and well off members of the civil society.

Requirements

Human Resources

We have a passionate volunteer team of lecturers and professors for video lectures of intermediate classes but for lectures of matric classes, we need to hire teachers from market. We are looking for support from the heads o f institutions Since they have complete data of their students, we therefore request them to provide links of video lectures to their students.

Logistic Support

To continue our volunteer activities we need a permanent office of SYMS, internet facility, PCs, laptops, printer, scanner, micro phones, digital notepads, video cameras, tripods etc.

Support of Institutional Head

We are looking for support from the heads of institutions. Since they have complete data of their students, we therefore request them to provide links of video lectures to their students.

Recognition

Furthermore, we are also looking for registration of this society so that we can present ourselves as registered organization to have confidence of the colleges and students and can carry out activities in colleges with an ease.

Favorable outcomes of SYMS mission

For Bright future of our students,

  • we focus on; Changing the course of study from confusion to vision
  • Changing minds from having problems to solving problems.
  • Changing ourselves From blaming others to having self accountability and taking responsibilities.
  • Setting the student free from financial barriers so that they can break the barriers of inventions.
