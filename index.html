Society of Young Mathematical Scientists (SYMS)

Society of Young Mathematical Scientists (SYMS) is mainly established for the students of Pakistan and especially Balochistan. The reason for society title is: SYMS team believes that a person who has a good knowhow of Math can become a great scientist in his field since this subject possesses its key role in all sciences.

Vision

The vision of the societ is to facilitate true vision of science and mathematics based on research and creativity, speed up the interactions among educational institutions, council students in choosing their career path and improve their aptitude and natural potentials.

Mission

We aim to support our students in overcoming the hurdles during their educational career. To achieve our objectives, we would act through experts in their relevant fields engaging our respected teachers and students in inter and intra institutional quiz competitions, speech contests, science exhibitions, workshops, seminars etc. to exploit their intrinsic abilities. We would also conduct awareness programs for helping underprivileged students, guide them to reach out local and international scholarship programs, provide free books, and pay their educational dues etc. In the similar context, we would be inviting dignitaries belonging to various walks of life, in the aforesaid programs and events, to encourage our cause.
